Voting underway for fifth phase of UP polls

Voting for the fifth phase of the union parishad (UP) polls is underway in Bangladesh, with over 36,400 candidates in the fray for different posts, UNB reports. Polling began in 708 UPs across 95 upazilas in 48 districts at 8:00am Wednesday and will continue till 4:00pm. A total of 36,457 candidates, including 3,278 contenders for the post of chairman, are contesting in the countrywide fifth-phase UP election.
